What is Only a Survey.com?

OnlyASurvey.com is an accessible online survey website. Our surveys are fully CLF 2, 508 and W3C-WCAG  priority 1 and priority 2 compliant.  Our survey builder is fully accessible and  is W3C-WCAG  priority 1 and priority 2 compliant an industry first.  So anyone in your organization can now create and deploy accessible surveys in minutes.

 We believe what should be hard about conducting a survey should be coming up with great questions not trying to figure out how to use your survey tool and determine if it is accessible to the disabled, compliant with various government standards and secure.

For this reason we created Only a Survey.com, the accessible, easy to use and secure survey tool.

Benefits:

  • simple to use
  • accessible to all
  • compliant with CLF 2, 508, W3C-WCAG (priority 1 and 2) and EU standards for accessibility
  • secure
  • easy to export your data to Excel for complex analysis
