Use a page on your site as a template

By using a page on your website as a template you have full control of how your survey will look. Our Magic Template Integration™ process will grab your page and wrap your survey with it.

Pick an integration method

Option 1 - Auto-detect my template

If your web site uses a Government of Canada CLF 2 template we can auto-detect your sites look and feel and insert your survey in to it.

Option 2 - Insert our hidden tags in to a page on your web site

If your web site is not a Government of Canada CLF 2 web site we can still wrap your survey with the look and feel of your web site.

Just add these two HTML comments to a page on your web site

<!-- OAS01 -->

<!-- OAS02 -->

Any content between these two tags will be replaced with your survey.
