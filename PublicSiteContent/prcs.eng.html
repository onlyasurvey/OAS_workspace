Features

All of our surveys offer:

  • Fully WCAG 2 and 508 compliant surveys and survey builder
  • Email invites
  • Advanced branching
  • Export to Excel

Supported Question Types

All of our accounts support these question types.

  • Page/Section (HTML)
  • Text Field (Also known as short-form open text questions)
  • Essay (Also known as long-form open text questions)
  • Radio Buttons (select only one)
  • Check Boxes (select multiple)
  • Select List (select only one)
  • Rating Scale
  • Constant Sum
