About Us

Only A Survey was created to allow any organization to create and deploy accessible and secure surveys using a simple accessible interface.

Why we started:

Designed by a UX expert in the area of accessible interface design OAS generates fully accessible surveys and has a fully accessible survey builder which no other survey platform offers at any price on the market today.

OAS is the simple, accessible and secure survey tool which can be used by anyone with or without a disability.

Only A Survey is:

  • simple to use
  • accessible to all - including admin interfaces
  • compliant with WCAG 2, 508 and EU standards for accessibility
  • secure
  • easy to export your data to Excel for complex analysis

We hope you enjoy using it as much as we enjoyed creating it!
