Regulations and Legislation

The following acts, orders and other acts of interest govern Canada’s inland and marine fisheries, aquatic species, oceans, habitat, fishing and recreational harbours, and marine services fees.

For a complete list of acts, orders and regulations, refer to Justice Canada.

Read the disclaimer regarding any references to legal materials on this site.
