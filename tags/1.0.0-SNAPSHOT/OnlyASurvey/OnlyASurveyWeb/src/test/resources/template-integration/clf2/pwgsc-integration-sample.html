Accountability and Transparency

Public Works and Government Services Canada continuously strives to be transparent and accountable in how it does business. Here you will find links to detailed departmental information on our operations, including Sponsorship, Proactive Disclosure, Access to Information and Privacy, Advertising, Transparent Contracting, Contract Claims Resolution and our Internal Audit and Review activities.

Our goals are to continue to improve how we manage and to operate with integrity.

Accountability

On April 11, 2006, the government introduced the Federal Accountability Act and its associated Action Plan in the House of Commons. This omnibus legislation is helping to strengthen accountability, and increase transparency and oversight in government operations. It was granted Royal Assent on December 12, 2006.

PWGSC is playing a special role in the implementation of the Act and the Action Plan in the areas of procurement, advertising and public opinion research.

The Management Accountability Framework (MAF) is intended to translate the vision of modern public service management into a set of management expectations. Each year, TBS evaluates departments against various MAF indicators and posts the corresponding MAF Assessments on its web site. PWGSC was evaluated in Round IV (2006-2007), and prepared a Management Response to TBS’ Round IV MAF Assessment. In 2005-2006, PWGSC conducted a MAF self-assessment.

Proactive Disclosure

The Government of Canada is working hard to improve the proactive disclosure of information so that Canadians are better able to hold Parliament, the government, and public sector officials accountable.

The following reports are available:

  • Disclosure of Contracts – Information on contracts over $10,000.
  • Disclosure of Travel and Hospitality Expenses – Information on travel and hospitality expenses for senior departmental officials.
  • Disclosure of Position Reclassifications – Information on the reclassification of occupied positions by PWGSC.
  • Disclosure of Grants and Contributions – Information on grants and contributions awarded by PWGSC.
  • Disclosure of Wrongdoings in the Workplace - Information on founded disclosures.

For information about proactive disclosure at all Government of Canada institutions, please visit the Treasury Board Secretariat’s proactive disclosure site.
